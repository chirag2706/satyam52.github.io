About IIT Tirupati

IIT Tirupati has highly qualified profs in all departments. Since I am from first year, I can’t comment much about how well the profs teach from second year, But the first year profs are really good. Again, here it depends on how much knowledge you can gain from your prof. There will be a lot of people saying the profs are not good and all that, but that is because they expect spoon-feeding or something of that sort. If one works hard and is really interested in the subject, the profs here will guide you to success. The academic structure here is very good.There is not too much dumping of new concepts. It goes steadily and slowly, so that one has ample time to participate in other activities as well.
